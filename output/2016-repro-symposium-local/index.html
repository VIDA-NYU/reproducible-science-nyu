    Symposium Home / Travel & Location Information
    NYU's Reproducibility Symposium will be held in the Jacobs Room, in The Center for Urban Science + Progress at 1 MetroTech Center, 19th Floor, Brooklyn, NY 11201.

    Transportation Information

    Map of Campus (pdf) -- the Symposium is being held in CUSP, indicated with (1) on this map.

    • A C F train to MetroTech
    • 2 3 4 5 train to Borough Hall (walk one block East to Willoughby Street and make a left onto Jay Street)
    • M R train to Lawrence Street-MetroTech (walk one block North on Lawrence Street)
